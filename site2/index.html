2 Cavan Road

(Kwong Soon & Co. Engineering Work)





2 Cavan Road is a site with conserved building that houses the former Kwong Soon & Co. Engineering Works. The two art-deco building is built around the 1950s as a warehouse and ship repair workshop. Back then, Jalan Besar is still a region with active light industrial activities.
