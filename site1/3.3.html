The Contemporary Gateway

Proposed by: XYZ



Function: Commercial Shophouse


The proposal is modified from Model 6. This proposal retains the irregular block placement of Model 6 and uses it as an opportunity to introduce balconies and overhangs that is a contemporary interpretation of the adjacent building balconies. The rooftop of the building is used as an outdoor garden for the enjoyment of the tenants. More importantly, the ground level of the proposal leaves two semi-outdoor passageway that acts as a thoroughfare to the alley behind. This further reacts to the original use of the site as a gateway that leads to the former New World Amusement Park.
