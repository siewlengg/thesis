Participatory Planning: Survey 2

253 Jalan Besar

(former entrance to New World Amusement Park)





253 Jalan Besar was previously an empty plot serving as the main entrance to the New World Amusement Park established in 1923. The New World Amusement Park was closed in 1987 and the site was subsequently developed into the City Square Residences. The original gate was moved to the City Green park near to the City Square Mall.
