Participatory Planning Survey 2


The thesis intends to experiment with the idea of a social-media form of participatory planning process where normal citizens can participate in the discussion and design of a new development within designated heritage conservation area without being limited by their time schedule and location. This survey will observe how these discussions on design proposals are carried out and how the result of these discussions will lead to the modification of an existing design as well as a shift in heritage conservation approach.


The survey consists of 3 different sites located in the Jalan Besar Conservation Area.
Please spend some time to look through the proposals and leave comments as guests on the discussion section regarding one or a few of the following issues:

1.) Do you think this proposal should not be built on site? Why?
2.) What can you suggests to improve the design proposal?
3.) What other design would you suggest for the site?
4.) Do you think this building should or should not be conserved? What aspect of the building is allowed to be changed if it were to be conserved?
