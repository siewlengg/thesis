12-20 Penhas Road

(Residential Building with Commercial at First Storey)





12-20 Penhas Road is a mixed-use building block that comprises of 5 units of late-modern shophouses currently used as residential building with commercial at first storey. The high ceiling-height of the first storey shoplots is a strong architectural indicator of the light industrial activities that were prevalent in the history of the Jalan Besar region. The site is currently not under any conservation status.
